meeting → meet
was → be

- form

- Return ONLY CSV

form,model_lemma

lemma
NA

Use only the attached CSV file. Do not browse the web, search for external sources, retrieve outside documents, or cite external information. Do not create widgets or other tools for this project. Just label the data and output it.
